Important Notes

Every score is a weighted average across seven dimensions — writing quality, emotional impact, engagement & pacing, performances, rewatchability, critical consensus, and audience reception — drawing on reputation-weighted critics and calibrated against reference films at each tier. It's a researched, consistent yardstick, not a casual rating. That said, taste is personal: even a rigorous score can't predict what you will love. If a pick doesn't land for you, that's the gap between aggregate quality and individual taste — not a flaw in either. 🍿
When multiple films share the same title, this database always prefers the Indian release.
The Gautham Score is a recommendation tool, not an objective measure of quality. Scores may be revised as more reviews become available.
